3-Day Golf Itinerary
Day 1 · Wednesday Aug 13
Fly in + The Bear (Nicklaus)
Fly BDL → DTW → TVC. Check in to lakefront rental. 2pm tee at The Bear — Jack Nicklaus design at Grand Traverse Resort, 28-time host of the Michigan Open, with tiered greens and deep pot bunkers. Dinner downtown on Front Street.
Day 2 · Thursday Aug 14
The Wolverine + Wine Country
7:30am tee at The Wolverine (Gary Player design, same resort). Afternoon: explore Old Mission Peninsula wine trail — 20+ tasting rooms within 30 miles. Evening: Low Bar speakeasy or 7 Monks Taproom in downtown TC.
Day 3 · Friday Aug 15
Antrim Dells + Food & Wine Fest
8am tee time at Antrim Dells (Golf Digest 4.5★, former Michigan Amateur host). Afternoon: Sleeping Bear Dunes or Grand Traverse Bay beach. Evening: Food & Wine Festival events — chef dinners, winery tastings, live music across the region.
Day 4 · Saturday Aug 16
Morning round + Departure
Optional sunrise round at Spruce Run or replay at discount rate. TVC → DTW → BDL afternoon flights.

3-Day Golf Itinerary

Fly in + Cog Hill Dubsdread
Direct BDL→ORD. 12pm tee at Cog Hill Course 4 (Dubsdread) — longtime BMW Championship host, one of the premier public courses in the country. Dinner: Gibson's Bar & Steakhouse (iconic Chicago institution). Nightcap in River North.
Day 2 · Thursday Aug 14
The Glen Club + Wrigley
8am tee at The Glen Club — Tom Fazio masterpiece on Chicago's North Shore, consistently ranked top 5 in Illinois. Afternoon: optional Cubs game at Wrigley Field (August home game — check schedule). Evening: Wicker Park bar crawl.
Day 3 · Friday Aug 15
Harborside + Final City Night
8am tee at Harborside International (spectacular 36-hole facility with Chicago skyline views from the course). Afternoon: Chicago Riverwalk, Navy Pier, deep dish pizza at Lou Malnati's. Big final night — start in West Loop, end in River North clubs.
Day 4 · Saturday Aug 16
Brunch + Departure
Brunch at an iconic Chicago spot (Girl & the Goat or Au Cheval). ORD→BDL afternoon flight.

3-Day Golf Itinerary
Day 1 · Wednesday Aug 13
Fly in + Torrey Pines North
Morning departure BDL, arrive SAN by noon (West Coast). Check in to La Jolla rental. 3:30pm tee at Torrey Pines North — the more scenic of the two, dramatic canyon and ocean views. Sunset dinner in La Jolla Cove area. Drinks on Prospect Street.
Day 2 · Thursday Aug 14
Torrey Pines South — Bucket List Day
7am tee at Torrey Pines South — 2008 and 2021 US Open host, Tiger Woods won here on a broken leg. Walk the same fairways as the world's best players. 5.5 hour round expected. Afternoon: Coronado Island beach. Evening: Gaslamp Quarter bar crawl.
Day 3 · Friday Aug 15
Omni La Costa + Pacific Beach
8am tee at Omni La Costa North Course — recently renovated, host of NCAA Championships, plush SoCal resort conditions. Afternoon: Mission Beach or Pacific Beach boardwalk. Final night: craft brewery scene in North Park, then downtown Gaslamp.
Day 4 · Saturday Aug 16
Morning + Departure
Optional early round at Coronado Golf Course ($60, next to Hotel del Coronado). SAN → BDL evening flight. Long travel day — plan for red-eye or connecting flights.
