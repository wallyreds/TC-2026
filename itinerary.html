Lakefront home — unpack, assign rooms, grab groceries. Stock the house: beer, water, snacks, Gatorade. Costco or Meijer are both near TVC for a quick group supply run.

2:00pm
⛳
Round 1 — The Bear, Grand Traverse Resort
Jack Nicklaus design, ranked #33 in Michigan by Golf Digest 2025–26. 7,078 yards, slope 148, rating 76.3 — the toughest course on the trip. Scottish terraced fairways, deep pot bunkers, tiered greens, fruit orchards. Budget ~4.5–5 hrs. Book tee time via grandtraverseresort.com — public rate peak season runs ~$150–170 with cart.
Golf
7:30pm
🍽️
Dinner — Front Street, Downtown TC
Kick off the trip with a proper group dinner. Top picks for 8: Firefly Restaurant (upscale American, reservations recommended), North Peak Brewing Co. (craft beer + great pub food, lively, no reservation needed for groups), or Bourbons 72 (72 bourbons, tufted leather booths — perfect vibe).
Dinner
9:30pm
🍺
Night 1 — 7 Monks Taproom + Low Bar
Start at 7 Monks Taproom on Front St (46 taps, great pub energy). Then head downstairs to Low Bar, the award-winning speakeasy beneath it — 200+ rare spirits, dark moody atmosphere, exceptional cocktails. Early night — golf starts tomorrow.

Round 2 — The Wolverine, Grand Traverse Resort
Gary Player design on the same resort property — dramatically different feel from The Bear. More links-style with rolling fairways and fast greens. ~6,700 yards, highly ranked in Michigan. Slightly lower rate than The Bear (~$120–140 public peak). Book same day as The Bear via grandtraverseresort.com.
Golf
1:30pm
🚗
Afternoon — K1 Speed Indoor Go-Karts
Head to K1 Speed at Cherryland Center — all-electric Italian karts hitting 45mph on a professionally designed indoor track. Each guy gets timed laps so there's a real leaderboard. Arcade and Paddock Lounge on-site for anyone who wants to skip the driving. Great for groups of 8 — book ahead for reserved race times.
Go-Karts

Sunset at the rental — grill night
Cook at the house tonight — save money and enjoy the lakefront. Steaks or burgers on the grill, cold beers from the fridge, watch the sun go down over the bay. This is the best evening of the trip for many groups.

8:00am
⛳
Round 3 — Arcadia Bluffs (The Bluffs Course)
#16 in America's Top 100 Greatest Public Courses — Golf Digest 2025–26. Often called "Whistling Straits East," this links-style masterpiece sits on 245 acres of bluffs above Lake Michigan with 50 sod-walled bunkers, massive firm greens, and panoramic water views on nearly every hole. 7,300 yards, par 72. Plan for a 5-hour round — worth every minute. ~1 hr 10 min south of TC on the scenic Lake Michigan coast. Peak season rate ~$215/person. Book via arcadiabluffs.com or call (231) 889-3001 — groups of 8 may need the group coordinator.
Golf · Top 20 in America
2:00pm
🏖️
Post-round lunch at Arcadia Bluffs + drive back north
Have lunch in the Arcadia Bluffs clubhouse — panoramic Lake Michigan views, full menu. Then take the scenic drive back north along US-31 through Frankfort and Sleeping Bear Dunes country. Optional stop: Empire Beach or Sleeping Bear Dunes overlook en route back to TC — adds only 20 min.
Scenic Drive
7:00pm
🍽️
Final Dinner — Rare Bird Brewpub or Pepe Nero
Rare Bird Brewpub — top-rated TC brewpub, excellent food, energetic atmosphere, great for a large group and highly rated for group dining. Pepe Nero — upscale Italian on Front St, stunning pastas and branzino, if you want the fancy sendoff dinner. Book 3–4 weeks ahead for either.
Final Dinner
9:00pm
🎶
Big Final Night — Front Street bar crawl
TC's Front Street is walkable and packed on Saturday nights in August. 🎵 Pro tip: If you timed your Arcadia Bluffs tee time right, you may have caught the famous bagpiper — plays Thu–Sun at sunset on the 18th hole. Hit Union Street Station (live music since 1891), Taproot Cider House (Michigan-made ciders, great late-night spot), and end at The Filling Station Microbrewery (train depot setting, one of TC's most beloved bars). Late night — nobody has early tee times tomorrow.

Bars & Nightlife
Where to Go After Dark
🍸
Speakeasy · Night 1
Low Bar
Award-winning underground speakeasy beneath 7 Monks Taproom. 200+ rare spirits, expertly crafted cocktails, dark and moody atmosphere. One of the best bars in Michigan — don't miss it.
🍺
Taproom · Night 1
7 Monks Taproom
46 beers on tap, excellent pub food, lively crowd — the anchor of downtown TC nightlife. Perfect first stop before heading downstairs to Low Bar.
🥃
Distillery · Night 2
Traverse City Whiskey Co.
Local craft distillery tasting room on Front St. Michigan-grain whiskeys, cocktails, a must for whiskey fans. Great way to start Night 2 before heading to The Little Fleet.
🚚
Outdoor Bar · Night 2
The Little Fleet
Downtown outdoor bar surrounded by rotating food trucks and live music nightly in summer. Laid-back, fun, perfect group atmosphere. One of TC's most beloved spots.
🎸
Live Music · Night 3
Union Street Station
Live music in Traverse City since 1891. Local and regional acts, classic bar atmosphere, open late on weekends. The kind of place where great trips end.
🍎
Cider Bar · Night 3
Taproot Cider House
Michigan-made ciders in a warm, rustic setting. Excellent late-night spot for groups — great for those who want something different from beer. Consistently top-rated for group dining.
🚂
Brewpub · Any Night
The Filling Station
Set in a converted train depot — one of TC's most beloved bars. Great craft beer, unique setting, excellent for a big group. A TC institution not to be skipped.
🌿
Go-Karts · Day 2
Old Mission Peninsula
K1 Speed at Cherryland Center — electric karts to 45mph, timed laps, arcade and lounge on-site. Perfect for settling who the real driver of the group is.
Trip Logistics
Getting There & Around
✈ Flights
Best option: BOS → TVC nonstop (American/Delta/JetBlue, seasonal, ~2.5 hrs). Check availability immediately — fills up in August.
Backup: BDL → DTW → TVC via Delta (~4.5 hrs). Reliable, frequent.
Est. round trip: $280–$380 per person from BOS or BDL.
Book by: June 2026 at the latest. August is peak TC season — TVC is a small airport.
🏠 Accommodation
Lakefront Airbnb rental — avg $400–450/night for 4BR, split 8 ways = ~$160/person for 3 nights.
3 Airbnb options pre-identified — book ASAP, TC rentals go fast for Aug weekends.
Aim for Old Mission Peninsula or West Bay area for best lakefront access.
🚗 Getting Around TC
Rental cars: 2 SUVs split 8 ways. Book through the airport — August inventory is tight. Reserve now.
Downtown: Walkable for bars and restaurants once you're parked at the rental.
Golf courses: Grand Traverse Resort is 10 min from downtown TC. Arcadia Bluffs is ~1 hr 10 min south on US-31.
K1 Speed: Located at Cherryland Center in Garfield Township — 10-min drive from downtown TC. Book race times in advance for groups of 8+.
📅 Timing Notes
K1 Speed booking: Walk-ins welcome but groups of 8+ should reserve — visit k1speed.com or call the Traverse City location.
August weather: Avg 76°F, low humidity, sunny. Ideal golf conditions. Occasional afternoon thunderstorm — build a buffer in tee times if possible.
